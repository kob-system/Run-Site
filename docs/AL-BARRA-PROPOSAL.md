# 📄 Al Barra — Growth Retainer Proposal (draft)

> Client: Al Barra. Businesses: **Mega Troy Laundromat** + **Five Star Deli** (gas-station restaurant).
> Ask: run their social media + content + marketing ("crazy" content, filming, posting).
> Goal of this doc: scope + price it so it's PROFITABLE and FITS JP's time. Last updated 2026-05-29.

## The core principle
JP has a full-time job. The **only** part that requires his physical presence is **filming**. Everything else
(strategy, captions, scheduling, posting, reporting) is **batched or AI-automated**. So the offer is built around
**ONE monthly shoot per business**, then an automated content engine the rest of the month.

## Monthly workflow (how it actually runs)
1. **1 batch shoot per business / month** (on-site, ~1–2 hrs each) → capture 20–30 pieces of raw content.
2. **Editing** → freelance editor (delegate) or AI-assisted.
3. **Captions + calendar** → AI-drafted during JP's car/downtime, JP approves.
4. **Scheduling/posting** → GoHighLevel / scheduler posts automatically.
5. **Monthly report** → simple results recap.

## Package options (per business, or bundle the two)

### Good — "Presence" — ~$1,000/mo per business
- 1 shoot/mo, 12 posts/mo, captions + hashtags, scheduled posting, 1 platform focus.

### Better — "Growth" — ~$1,750/mo per business  ⭐ recommended
- 1–2 shoots/mo, 20 posts/mo, 2 platforms, stories/reels, review-request automation, monthly report.

### Best — "Domination" — ~$2,500+/mo per business
- 2 shoots/mo, 30+ posts, multi-platform, paid ads management, promotions/offers, full GHL marketing automation.

### 🤝 Bundle (both businesses)
Offer the pair at a slight discount to lock in volume — e.g. **Better tier for both = ~$3,000/mo** (vs $3,500 separate).
This is JP's anchor offer.

## What's included / NOT included
**Included:** strategy, content capture (scheduled shoots), editing, captions, scheduling, posting, reporting.
**Not included (add-ons):** paid ad spend (client pays the platforms), daily/event filming beyond the monthly shoot,
website changes (separate Care Plan), responding to every DM in real time (can add a VA later).

## Profit math (why the price works)
At ~$1,750/mo per business: budget ~$300–500/mo for a freelance editor, keep the rest. JP's hands-on time =
~2 hrs filming + a few hours of AI-assisted approval/oversight. **High margin, time-bounded, scalable.**

## ⚠️ Before saying yes
- Confirm Al Barra's **budget** and that he'll **pay monthly up front** (auto-pay).
- Get it in **writing**: scope, shoot cadence, what's extra.
- Start with **ONE business or the Better tier** to prove the system before scaling to both at max.
- Decide the editor plan BEFORE committing (don't let editing fall on JP's evenings).
