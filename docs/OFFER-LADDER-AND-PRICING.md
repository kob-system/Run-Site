# 💰 Offer Ladder & Pricing

> Turns custom one-off chaos into repeatable products. Capital Region NY small-business market, AI-built, solo operator.
> Numbers are recommended STARTING points — raise them as your portfolio/confidence grows. Last updated 2026-05-29.

## The model
**Goal = recurring revenue.** Win the client with a fast, cheap, high-quality site (entry), then climb the ladder to monthly income. One-off cash is nice; **retainers are what make this survive next to a full-time job.**

## Pricing-model rule (setup + monthly — never $0 setup)
- **Setup fee = payment for the BUILD** (your labor + risk). It also filters tire-kickers.
- **Monthly = payment for ONGOING value.** Match the monthly to what's *actually* ongoing.
- ❌ **Never $0 setup** — you'd do all the heavy build up front and eat it if they cancel early.
- ❌ **Never a hosting-sized monthly ($100) while promising marketing-sized work** — that's the scope-creep trap ($99–$299/mo is the industry "red flag" zone).
- ✅ Website client → **$1,000 setup + $99/mo Care Plan** (light edits only; bigger work billed extra).
- ✅ "Everything" client → smaller/no site fee + **higher monthly** that genuinely contains the ongoing work.

---

## Tier 1 — Site-in-a-Day  *(entry / trust-builder)*
- **What:** Local-business website from your reusable template — branded, mobile, professional, live.
- **Scope:** up to ~5 sections (hero, services, about, reviews, contact), contact form, basic SEO, Google Map.
- **Price:** **$1,000–$1,500 flat.** (50% deposit up front, balance on launch.)
- **Your cost:** ~$0 (Vercel free/Pro). Mostly your time + my build → high margin.
- **Excludes:** copywriting from scratch (client provides info via intake brief), e-commerce, logins.

## Tier 2 — Care Plan  *(recurring — the real prize)*
- **What:** Hosting + monitoring + small monthly edits + "we keep it running."
- **Scope:** hosting, SSL, up to 30–60 min of edits/mo, uptime check, backups.
- **Price:** **$79–$99/mo** (annual option: 10 months for 12).
- **Your cost:** ~$0. **Near-pure margin recurring.** Attach this to EVERY site sold.

## Tier 3 — CRM Setup  *(project + recurring)*
- **What:** GoHighLevel build — pipeline, booking calendar, review requests, missed-call text-back, email/SMS follow-up automations.
- **Price:** **$750–$2,000 setup** + **$300–$800/mo** management (or client runs it themselves after setup).
- **Note:** if you white-label GHL later, the sub-account *is* your product — resell it.

## Tier 4 — Growth Retainer  *(high-ticket recurring — social/content/marketing)*
- **What:** Done-for-you social + content. **Batched + automated + editing delegated** (see Al Barra proposal).
- **Price:** **$1,000–$3,000/mo per business**, scaled to post volume + shoot frequency.
- **Rule:** price high enough to afford a freelance editor AND still profit. Never trade all your hours for this.

---

## Payment & scope rules (protect yourself)
1. **Always take a deposit** (50% on builds). No deposit, no work.
2. **Retainers billed monthly, up front.** Auto-pay via GHL.
3. **Scope in writing.** Anything outside scope = change order / extra.
4. **You own the code (GitHub); client gets the live site + a license.** Never hand over a dependency you can't rebuild (the Base44 lesson).
5. **Bundle on the way in:** every site = "+ $89/mo Care Plan?" Every Care Plan = "+ CRM?"
