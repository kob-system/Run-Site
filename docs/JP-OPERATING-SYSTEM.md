## §3. Strategy
**Positioning:** *"The AI-powered local growth partner for Capital Region small businesses."*

**Niche down** to verticals he already has proof in: **laundromats, car washes, restaurants/delis, trades/maintenance.** Same templates + same playbook = compounding speed and referrals within tight local networks.

**Offer ladder (productize the chaos):**
1. **Site-in-a-Day** — flat-fee local business site from the reusable template. *Entry / trust-builder.*
2. **Care Plan** — monthly hosting + maintenance + small edits. *Recurring revenue.* ← the goal
3. **CRM Setup** — GHL pipelines, booking, follow-up automation. *Project + recurring.*
4. **Growth Retainer** — social/content/marketing, **batched + automated + priced to delegate editing.** *High-ticket recurring.*

**North star:** shift from one-off project income → **recurring monthly revenue** (Care Plans + retainers). Recurring is what makes the side business survivable alongside a full-time job.
