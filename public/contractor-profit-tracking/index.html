Profit tracking — FAQ

How do contractors track profit on a job?

Log every cost tied to the job — labor, materials, subs, mileage — and compare it to what you charged. JobTally automates the logging (GPS hours, scanned receipts) and shows live projected profit per job.

Can I see if a job is making money before it's finished?

Yes. Each job shows price against cost-to-date as the work runs, so a job trending toward a loss is visible that week — while you can still do something about it.

Do I need an accountant to do job costing?

No. JobTally is built so an owner-operator can watch per-job margins without an accountant or a spreadsheet. Your accountant still handles taxes.
