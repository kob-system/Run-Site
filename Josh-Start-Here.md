# Run-Site — Quick Start (for Josh)

**What it is:** a simple app to track each job's money and your crew's hours — right from your phone. No spreadsheets. (This is an early version I'm building, so your honest feedback is the whole point.)

---

### 1. Open it on your phone
Go to: **runsite-pearl.vercel.app**
👉 Tip: tap your browser's **Share → "Add to Home Screen"** so it sits on your phone like a real app.

### 2. Make your account
Tap **Sign Up** → pick **"Contractor / Owner"** → enter your name, company, email, and a password. Done.

### 3. Add a job
Tap **+ New Job** and fill in:
- **Job name** (e.g. "18 Dutch Village")
- **Client name**
- **Materials $** — what you expect to spend on materials
- **Labor $** — what you expect to pay your crew
- **Profit $** — what you want to make
The app adds those up into your total price.

### 4. Snap your receipts
Open a job → **Receipts → + Add Receipt → take a photo.** It reads the store and amount for you. Your **profit updates live** as the money goes out.

### 5. Add your crew (new — easier now)
Open the **Workers** tab → **Invite a worker** → type their name → **Copy link** → **text it to them.** They tap the link, set a password, and they're instantly linked to you — no typing your email, no mix-ups.

Once they're in, on **their** phone they can only do four things (their hours, never your numbers):
- **Clock In / Clock Out** — grabs their location; hours + cost flow straight into the job.
- **See their schedule** — the days/jobs you've assigned them.
- **Request time off** — pick dates + a reason; it lands in your **Workers** tab to **approve or deny.**
- That's it — they never see your profit or margins.

You can also **Remove** a worker from the Workers tab; the hours they already logged stay on your jobs.

### 6. Add photos from the job
Open a job → **Photos** → you or your crew can add a shot **from the camera OR straight from the phone's photo library** (not just brand-new photos).

### 7. See your numbers
The **Reports** tab shows profit per job and a one-tap **export for taxes**.
